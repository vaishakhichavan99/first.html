Vision:

To create and develop comitted, knowledgeable, qualifide and excellent world class Badminton coaches

Mission

To regulate, promote, manage and develop the sport of 'Badminton' in the district transparently. To create comprehensively established progressive pathway for badminton players, coaches and other technical person of district.".

Aims & Object:

To encourage, standarise,control,promote and develop the game of Badminton in district especially by organising Coaching, Training Camps, Tournaments and Exhibition Matches amongest Schools, Colleges, Universities and States through and by affiliated Organisations on an amateur basis for adults, juniors and sub-juniors.

To maintain general control of Badminton in district particularly with referance to the Rules and Regulation for the conduct of the Tournaments.

To act as 'Accredited Representative'of all its affiliated Organisations in all matters of common interest as District Body.

To co-ordinate District , Inter-District & State activities respect of'Badminton' in all ways including holding of Disrict,Inter- Disrict & Inter-State Tournaments,organising or recognising Championship
Tournaments/ Matches.

To organise District Championships of 'Badminton' in Buldana for enhancing overall status of badminton in the District and to set path for 'Olympic Qualification' by the players.

To maintain a panel of 'Coaches' and also to nominate National Coaches of Senior and Juniors.

To support,populirise and encourage the development of 'Badminton' as a sports for all.
